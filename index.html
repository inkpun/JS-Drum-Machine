Instructions


On the Pads

Each pad represents a 16th note. And its corresponding instrument is labeled on the left. When the pad is gray, it's turned off. And no sound will play on that beat. Clicking a gray pad (sound off) will turn it on and trigger its sound.

When the pad is orange, it's turned on. And a sound will play on that beat. Clicking an orange pad (sound on) will turn it off without triggering its sound. You can toggle the pads even while the machine is currently playing or stopped.


On the BPM

The default beats per minute (BPM) is 90. Clicking on the – button decreases the BPM. While clicking on the + button increases it. You can also change the BPM regardless whether the machine is currently playing or stopped.


On the Play/Stop Button

By default, the machine is stopped. Clicking the ▶ PLAY button will start the beat. On the other hand, clicking the ⏹ STOP button will stop it. Note that no sound will play if all the pads are gray. Or no pad is orange.
